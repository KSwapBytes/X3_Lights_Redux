# X3-Lights / Cheap Lights

Save-compatible Satisfactory 1.2 / SML 3.12 continuation of the original X3-Lights mod created by [x3008x](https://github.com/Rick86). [Acxd](https://github.com/Acxd25) ported the mod for Satisfactory Update 5, and [KSwapBytes](https://github.com/KSwapBytes) rebuilt it for Satisfactory 1.2.

The original asset mount and generated-class paths are retained so existing saves can resolve previously placed X3 lights.

## Buildings

| Building | Build cost | Power | Power links |
| --- | --- | ---: | ---: |
| X3 Street Light | 2 Iron Plates, 12 Iron Rods, 2 Wire, 2 Cable | 0.2 MW | 4 |
| X3 Ceiling Light | 6 Iron Plates, 8 Iron Rods, 8 Wire, 2 Cable | 0.5 MW | 4 |
| X3 Flood Light Tower | 6 Concrete, 12 Iron Plates, 24 Iron Rods, 20 Wire, 4 Cable | 1.8 MW | 4 |
| X3 Wall-Mounted Flood Light | 6 Iron Plates, 6 Iron Rods, 12 Wire, 2 Cable | 0.9 MW | 4 |
| X3 Light Control Panel | 8 Iron Plates, 4 Iron Rods, 10 Wire, 6 Cable | — | 4 downstream |

All buildings unlock from the X3-Lights Tier 2 milestone.
